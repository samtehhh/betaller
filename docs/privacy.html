Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

BeTaller ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

BeTaller stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Name, age, gender, height, and weight
  • Height measurement records
  • Daily routine completion status
  • Water and sleep tracking data
  • App preferences and language settings

Third-Party Services

BeTaller does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using SharedPreferences on your device. If you uninstall the app, all data will be permanently deleted.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Since all information is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any server after deletion because no data is ever transmitted to external servers.

GDPR & International Users

BeTaller does not collect, transmit, or process any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device and is never shared with any third party. As a result, BeTaller is compliant with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and similar international privacy laws.

Children's Privacy

BeTaller is designed for users of all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, no personal data leaves the device.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact.betaller@gmail.com
